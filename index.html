📖 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Navigate your glowing cube through each level and reach the green exit portal before time runs out. Progress through increasingly difficult levels to prove your mastery!

⌨️ Keyboard Controls

←→Move Left / Right
SpaceFlip Gravity (toggle ceiling/floor)
RRestart Current Level

📱 Mobile / Touch Controls

←→D-pad buttons to move
↑Flip gravity UP (ceiling)
↓Flip gravity DOWN (floor)

⚖️ Gravity Mechanics

Press Space to instantly flip gravity. Your cube will fall upward to the ceiling or back down to the floor. Use this to dodge obstacles, navigate platforms, and reach the exit portal. Timing is everything!

⏱️ Timer & 🪙 Coins

Each level has a countdown timer. Complete the level before it hits zero! Finishing quickly earns you bonus coins based on remaining time. If you run out of time, spend 100 🪙 to buy +10 seconds.

💥 Hazards & Death

Red spikes — instant death on contact!
Orange moving blocks (Level 7+) — deadly moving obstacles that bounce around.
Falling off-screen — if your cube leaves the play area, you die.

💡 Pro Tips

• Flip gravity before you reach a spike to soar over it.
• Platforms on the ceiling are just as useful as floor platforms!
• Speed = more coins. Don't be cautious, be fast.
• Later levels limit your gravity flips — plan each flip carefully!
• Use R to quickly restart if you get stuck.
